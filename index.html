Real Patient Transformations

See the real results of our treatments — verified clinical cases from Dantisht.

🦷 Complete Denture

From Missing Teeth to a Full, Confident Smile

Concern: The patient had multiple missing teeth causing difficulty in chewing and low self-confidence.

Treatment: Custom-made complete dentures were fabricated to restore full dental function and aesthetics.

Result: The patient regained the ability to eat comfortably and smile confidently in social settings.

🩺 Infection Treatment

Pain-Free After Emergency Abscess Care

Concern: The patient experienced severe tooth pain and facial swelling due to a periapical abscess.

Treatment: Emergency drainage was performed, followed by a pulpectomy procedure to remove the infected nerve tissue.

Result: Complete resolution of infection and pain, saving the natural tooth from extraction.

✨ Full Mouth Rehabilitation

A Complete Smile Makeover — Inside and Out

Concern: Severely worn, damaged, and misaligned teeth affecting both function and appearance.

Treatment: A comprehensive full mouth rehabilitation combining crowns, bridges, and cosmetic procedures.

Result: A completely transformed smile with restored bite function and a dramatically improved appearance.

🔧 Fixed Bridge

Bridging the Gap for a Seamless Smile

Concern: Missing teeth in the visible smile zone causing gaps and difficulty chewing.

Treatment: A custom-designed fixed dental bridge was placed to fill the gap using adjacent teeth as support.

Result: A natural-looking, permanent restoration that blends seamlessly with the patient's existing teeth.

👶 Children's Treatment

Little Teeth, Big Transformation

Concern: Early childhood dental decay affecting multiple primary teeth.

Treatment: Gentle, child-friendly restorations using tooth-colored fillings and stainless steel crowns.

Result: Healthy, pain-free teeth restored with a positive dental experience for the child.
